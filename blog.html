WHAT WE DO


Acting as a ‘finger on the design pulse’ we identify what is new and cutting-edge,

and translate it into persuasive inspiration and products for the home.


We complete a decade of celebrating modern India as a vibrant design destination.


Our collections marry form with functionality,

easy to mix and match pieces to imbue your home and workspaces with warmth and energy.




WHO WE ARE


Based in the gritty industrial heart of Mumbai, Wardrobe B is a Design and Color strategy studio. Our niche consulting practice has grown into joyous home stores in Bengaluru, Goa, Mumbai, and an online presence. We bring to you, versatile pieces that celebrate the modern Indian sensibility that has a global appeal.

WHY WE'RE DIFFERENT


At Wardrobe B, we design with Joy, no pretense, and dollops of relevance. Bold and graphic in colour, there’s always something unique and unexpected—a thumbprint against standardization. Designs that speak to the global citizen and are at home anywhere in the world.
